Main category: cs.CL

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: The authors evaluate four prompting paradigms—Zero-Shot, Few-Shot, Zero-Shot Chain-of-Thought, and Few-Shot Chain-of-Thought—on three models (GPT-3.5, GPT-4, GPT-4o) using the ChartQA dataset. These insights can guide practitioners in choosing prompts that balance efficiency and accuracy for structured data reasoning in practical applications.
